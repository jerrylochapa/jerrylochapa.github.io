Brand Guide

  • Concept: As a fan of Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) my website is for a gym that teaches MMA in person and or online through a subscription program. Typically, MMA gyms are very expensive and this gym aims to be cheaper than the competition.

  • Mission Statement: Have a positive impact in our members lives through the teachings of martial arts. We have passionate and reputable instructors that promote our students to reach their goals, whether it be for fitness or to compete professionally.
